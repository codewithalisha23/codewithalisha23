Our Story

Waffelicious Delights started with a simple idea in a small kitchen: why choose between a warm dessert and a cold one? In 2018, our founder, Clara Sweet, perfected the art of the light, crispy waffle and paired it with her grandmother's secret recipe for smooth, slow-churned ice cream.

We believe in using only the finest ingredients: real vanilla beans, fresh seasonal berries, and locally sourced dairy. Our mission is to spread joy, one perfectly balanced Waffle & Ice Cream combination at a time.

"Quality ingredients and happy customers are the secret sauce to our success." - Clara Sweet, Founder.
